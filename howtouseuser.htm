How to use InNav

How to navigate

  1. Choose ‘Navigation’ in the main menu. You’ll be re-directed to the map-selection menu.
  2. Choose your building from the available list of buildings in the dropdown menu. Next, choose your floor from the dropdown menu. Click on ‘Navigate’ to go to the navigation portal.
  3. Once you are on the navigation portal, you will have the choice to do the following-
    • Find your way
      1. At the bottom of the map choose your current junction/ junction you want to start from in the ‘Source’ dropdown menu.
      2. Similarly, choose your destination/ junction you want to reach in the ‘Destination’ dropdown menu.
      3. Submit your selection. The path you need to follow will be displayed on the map.
    • Get information
        • You can get the relevant information about the junctions in your path and nearby services.
    • Report an issue
      1. You can file a complaint for any service, by clicking on the ‘Complaint’ button.
      2. A pop-up will appear on the screen, seeking details of the issue.
      3. Select the service you want to report from the dropdown menu.
      4. Choose the type of issue from the options given. In case your complaint is not listed, choose the ‘Other’ option. Now, write your complaint in the area which appears below the ‘Other’ option.
      5. Click on ‘Submit’ to submit your complaint.
